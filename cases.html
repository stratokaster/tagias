Taking pictures of shelves in grocery stores and annotating them with bounding boxes
Task:
It was required to take photos of shelves in grocery stores and label 5 categories of goods.
Tagias Services:
Data Collection and Bounding Boxes.
Workflow:
First, customer ordered collection of 200 pictures of product shelves for quality assessment. After quality approvement the customer placed a full order for collecting the entire dataset of 49,800 pieces with special requirement of excluding alcohol pictures. Work on the project took 7 days.
Result:
  • 1,620,000 labeled products on shelves in 5 categories
  • 50,000 unique photos of product shelves from 5 countries were collected specifically for the customer
  • All products in photos are marked with bounding boxes
Cost:
  • Collecting of 50,000 pictures, $0.04 each = $2,000.
  • 32.4 boxes per picture for 50,000 images, $0.01 per box = $16,200.
  • Processing of each of 50,000 pictures, $0.004 each = $200.
TOTAL:
$ 18,350
Annotating cars' pictures using polygons
Task:
It was required to label pictures of cars with polygons on a dataset of 14,000 photos that the customer provided.
Tagias Services:
Polygons
Workflow:
The customer provided a dataset of 14,000 photos. During the pre-moderation process it was revealed that 183 (1.3%) photos of entire dataset where related to accidents. After underlining the issue, the customer decided by to remove the pictures. All other images were annotated with polygons.
Result:
  • 129,220 cars marked with polygons
  • 14,000 photos were pre-checked for case relevance
Cost:
  • 14,000 photos were pre-moderated and evaluated for relevance, $0.004 each = $56
  • 129,220 cars were annotated with polygons, $0.025 each = $3,230.5
TOTAL:
$3,286.5
